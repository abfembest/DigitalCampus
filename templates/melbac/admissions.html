Welcome to MELBAC

A Free Education for the Kingdom

The Board of Management of Melchisedec Graduate Bible Academy, an admission free and tuition free non-denominational theological university, cheerfully welcomes intending students who are applying to study in this institution, and also the general public who are interested in knowing what this institution is made of, into our website.

Melchisedec Graduate Bible Academy (MELBAC) is an institution of graduate and post-graduate studies and is recognized by the Federal Government of Nigeria to award certificate, diploma, baccalaureate (bachelors), masters and doctorate degrees.

Universal Requirement for All Programs

All applicants for any degree level must be born again, baptized in water (immersion) and the Holy Ghost, and must have shown interest in becoming trained Ministers of the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Level 01

Basic Certificate

Requirements

  • All applicants must be born again, baptized in water (immersion) and the Holy Ghost, and must have shown interest in becoming trained Ministers of the gospel of Jesus Christ.
  • All applicants must be proficient in reading and writing.

Duration & Programs

1 Year · 2 Semesters

Programs Offered

  • Christian Ministry

Level 02

Advanced Certificate

Requirements

  • All applicants must be born again, baptized in water (immersion) and the Holy Ghost, and must have shown interest in becoming trained Ministers of the gospel of Jesus Christ.
  • All applicants must have high school equivalent or Secondary School Certificate with pass level.
  • All applicants must submit testimonials from previous schools attended.
  • All applicants must submit previous schools' transcripts and certificates.

Duration & Programs

1 Year · 2 Semesters

Programs Offered

  • Christian Ministry

Level 03

Diploma

Requirements

  • All applicants must be born again, baptized in water (immersion) and the Holy Ghost, and must have shown interest in becoming trained Ministers of the gospel of Jesus Christ.
  • All applicants must have 5 credits at no more than 2 sittings in WASC/SSCE, O'Level GCE or its equivalent.
  • All applicants must submit testimonials from previous schools attended.
  • All applicants must submit previous schools' transcripts and certificates.

Duration & Programs

1 Year · 2 Semesters

Programs Offered

  • Christian Ministry

Level 04

Bachelors Degree

Requirements

  • All applicants must be born again, baptized in water (immersion) and the Holy Ghost, and must have shown interest in becoming trained Ministers of the gospel of Jesus Christ.
  • All applicants must have a minimum of 5 credits at no more than 2 sittings in WASC/SSCE, O Level/GCE, or its equivalent. OR a Diploma certificate (OND/NCE/HND) from a recognized Theological/Bible school.

Duration

4 Years · 8 Semesters For WASC/SSCE, O Level/GCE applicants
2 Years · 4 Semesters For Diploma certificate (OND/NCE/HND) holders
