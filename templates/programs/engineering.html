Program Overview

Our Engineering program provides a solid foundation in engineering principles, mathematics, and applied sciences while offering specialized tracks in civil, mechanical, electrical, and chemical engineering. You'll work on real-world projects, participate in design competitions, and collaborate with industry partners.

Through hands-on laboratory work, design projects, and internship opportunities, you'll develop the technical expertise, problem-solving skills, and innovative thinking required to tackle complex engineering challenges and advance technology for societal benefit.

Learning Outcomes

  • Problem-solving and critical thinking
  • Project management and teamwork
  • Sustainable engineering practices
