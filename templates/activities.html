Ministry in Action

Student Activities

Our students don't just study the Word — they live it. Through hands-on ministry, they develop pastoral skills and deepen their understanding of Christian service.

01

Outreach Ministry

Prison and Hospital Ministry

Our students participate in prison and hospital visitations to apply their studies in real-world settings. These experiences provide opportunities to share their faith, offer spiritual support, and provide compassionate care to those in need. These ministries offer valuable hands-on experience, helping students develop their pastoral skills and deepen their understanding of ministry.

02

Compassionate Service

Widows and Orphanage Care

Students actively engage in Widows and Orphanage Care, embodying the biblical call to serve the vulnerable. This involvement takes many forms, from regular visits to local orphanages and care facilities, where students offer companionship, practical assistance, and spiritual encouragement, to organizing events to provide essential resources.

03

Elderly Outreach

Care for the Elderly

Students may provide companionship, read scripture, and offer prayer to elderly individuals in nursing homes or assisted living facilities. They often provide spiritual support to those who are dealing with issues related to the end of life. Essentially, our students demonstrate Christian love and compassion through practical service and spiritual support.
