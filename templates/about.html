Our Identity

Who We Are

Melchisedec International University is a globally recognized institution delivering high-quality, innovative education with flexibility and excellence.

Global Outlook, Local Impact

Founded in 1995, MIU has been at the forefront of digital education transformation, providing accessible, world-class education that empowers students to achieve their personal and professional goals, regardless of geographic location.

Our diverse community of learners, faculty, and staff from over 120 countries creates a rich, multicultural environment that prepares students for success in an interconnected world.

Our Journey

Our History

From our founding in 1995 to becoming a global education leader, discover MIU's journey of innovation and growth.

1995 - Founding

MIU is established with a vision to provide accessible, quality higher education through innovative delivery methods.

2005 - Digital Transformation

Launch of our first fully online degree programs, pioneering digital education.

2015 - Global Expansion

Establishment of international campuses in Europe and Asia, serving students from over 80 countries.

2020 - Innovation Hub

Opening of the MIU Innovation Center, fostering research and entrepreneurship.

2025 - Today

Recognized as a top global university with 25,000+ students and 120+ countries represented.
