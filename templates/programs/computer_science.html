Program Overview

Our Computer Science program provides a comprehensive foundation in computing theory, software development, and emerging technologies. You'll learn to design, develop, and optimize software systems while gaining expertise in areas like artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and cloud computing.

Through hands-on projects, internships, and industry partnerships, you'll build a strong portfolio and gain real-world experience that prepares you for success in the rapidly evolving tech industry.

  • Algorithm design and data structures
  • Software engineering best practices
  • AI and machine learning fundamentals
