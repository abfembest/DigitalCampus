Course Description

Strategic Business Management provides a comprehensive framework for analyzing, formulating, and implementing business strategies in today's dynamic global marketplace. This course bridges the gap between theory and practice, equipping students with the tools needed to make strategic decisions that create sustainable competitive advantage.

What You'll Learn

Analyze competitive environments using Porter's Five Forces
Develop and implement effective business strategies
Evaluate organizational capabilities and resources
Lead strategic change initiatives
Apply ethical decision-making frameworks
Create strategic plans for global expansion

Prerequisites
