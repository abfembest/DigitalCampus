Program Overview

Our Health Sciences program combines rigorous academic coursework with extensive clinical experience to prepare you for a fulfilling career in healthcare. You'll learn from experienced healthcare professionals and gain hands-on experience in state-of-the-art simulation labs and clinical settings.

Whether you're pursuing nursing, public health, healthcare administration, or pre-medical studies, our comprehensive curriculum emphasizes patient-centered care, evidence-based practice, and ethical decision-making in diverse healthcare environments.

Learning Outcomes

  • Clinical assessment and patient care
  • Evidence-based healthcare practice
  • Healthcare policy and ethics
  • Interprofessional collaboration
