Our Mission

Training Disciples of
the Lord Jesus Christ

The fundamental vision of Melchisedec Graduate Bible Academy according to the Lord's command is to train men and women who as disciples of our Lord Jesus, and part of God's end-time army, are dedicated to the service of God on the basis of "freely you receive, freely give."

The main objective of MELBAC is to teach its students the pure word of God — helping the faith of prospective ministers build on a solid foundation of true Christianity.

Living the Word

Our Student Activities

Students apply their studies in real-world ministry settings — embodying the Gospel through practical service.

Care for the Elderly

Students provide companionship, read scripture, and offer prayer to elderly individuals in nursing homes — demonstrating Christian love through practical and spiritual support.

Prison & Hospital Ministry

Our students participate in prison and hospital visitations to apply their studies in real-world settings, sharing faith and offering spiritual support to those in need.

Widows & Orphanage Care

Students actively engage in Widows and Orphanage Care, embodying the biblical call to serve the vulnerable through visits, practical assistance, and spiritual encouragement.
